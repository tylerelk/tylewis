Bio

I'm Ty.

I'm a web designer with a background in healthcare. I've spent years learning and growing with the goal of helping people. I've seen the world change before my eyes - technology has consumed our everyday and I'm here to embrace the change.

I'm offering my services as a web developer while working on my own projects to add efficiency and care to the ways we interact with tech. It's always been a core belief of mine that a noble goal is one that helps more people.

The attitude I take to designing digital products is always one of growth, learning, compassion, and care. As a nurse of almost a decade, I've learned the importants of assessing your situation and the challenge at hand, as well as how to systematically and efficiently overcome those challenges.

I am very much a product of my surroundings. Spending the majority of my life split between Southern California and New York City has offered me a unique aesthetic. Bold, clean lines and impactful headlines are countered by interesting textures and relaxing colors. I love pastels and deep tones, and have a great appreciation for classic design styles.

I especially love Art-Deco, Mid-Century, and Brutalist architecture and palettes.

Tools I use:

  • Javscript

  • Node.js

  • React
